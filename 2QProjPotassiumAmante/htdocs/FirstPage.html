Teenage Pregnancies are exactly what they sound like. It is when a woman below a certain age, specifically the age of 20 gets pregnant intentionally or not. You technically don't have to be a teen, a girl even 13 and below being pregnant will still be considered as a teenage pregnancy even if they aren't "teens". It is quite an unfortunate incident due to the many consequences that will drastically alter and possibly ruin the life of the pregnant woman and her child and there is even a whole different discussion about abortion that will only be slightly discussed
